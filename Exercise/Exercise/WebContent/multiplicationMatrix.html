Multiplication Matrix

Print the multiplication matrix. Such that for a input n. First row is 1*1 1*2 ... 1*n , second row is 2*1 2*2 ... 2*n and until n*1 ... n*n . Make sure there is a space after EVERY number.

Input : 3

Output:

1 2 3 

2 4 6

3 6 9

 

Input: 4

Output:

1 2 3 4 

2 4 6 8 

3 6 9 12 

4 8 12 16
