Name, Age, and Salary

 
Write a java program to ask the user for his/her name, age, and salary (double). Follow the input/output format. Following conversation should be displayed as output on screen, where you will enter the values of name,age and salary.

Suppose your inputs are:
     John
     22
     500

Expected Output:
     Hello. What is your name?
     Hi John! How old are you?
     So you're 22 eh? That's not old at all!
     How much do you make John?
     500.0! I hope that's per hour and not per year!
